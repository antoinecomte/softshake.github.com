Call for Papers is Open

Attention, closing date is July 27th 2014.

Few details about submission :

  • Talks can be submitted in French and English.
  • Each slot is one hour long but a session can take more than one slot if needed.
  • The format of the session is open (academic, interactive, workshop).
  • Speakers are kindly asked to provide their display support in order to provide them online to attendees after the event.
  • Accepted speakers will have free entrance to the conference. For talks with few speakers only one speaker will have entrance fee offered.
  • Given the tight budget of the conference we are sorry to be unable able to defray our speakers

You can submit your talk for one of the tracks (emails provided if you need any help):

  • Agile (track-agile@soft-shake.ch)
  • Big Data / NoSQL (track-bigdata@soft-shake.ch)
  • IA / Robotic (track-robotique@soft-shake.ch)
  • Java (track-java@soft-shake.ch)
  • Microsoft (track-microsoft@soft-shake.ch)
  • Mobility (track-mobile@soft-shake.ch)
  • Functional Programming (track-functional@soft-shake.ch)
  • Web [JS / CSS3 / HTML5] (track-web@soft-shake.ch)

And our Incubator track (track-incubator@soft-shake.ch) will hold anything that does not fit in other tracks.

For any questions regarding specific track, contact the owner of those track with the email refered above.

You can contact organisation team for any question at orga@soft-shake.ch.

Hope to see you soon in Geneva!
