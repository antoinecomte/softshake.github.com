Becoming a sponsor

 

Here are the different sponsorship packages and pricing :

 

  • "Cidre" at 500 CHF
    • Visibility on the website Sponsor's page (ogo)
    • Badge Sponsor
    • Name mentioned at the beginning during the plenary
    • Kakemono for a track (in the hall, A3 poster printed by Soft-Shake)
  • "Mousseux" at 2'000 CHF
    • Goodies to put in the bag of each attendee
    • Visibility on the buffet tables (poster)
    • Visibility on the Sponsors page (logo + description) and each page of the site (logo)
    • Visibility on the flyer
    • Word of the sponsor at the beginning during the plenary
  • "Champagne" at 4'000 CHF
    • Visibility on the homepage (logo)
    • Stand on the spot
    • Multitrack (mailing, kakemono)

A "Soft-Shake Sponsor" logo and a press kit will be available from the Multimedia Kit page.

These benefits will be enforced upon the payment reception by the Soft-Shake's association.

 

If you wish to sponsor this event, please contact us: mail@soft-shake.ch
