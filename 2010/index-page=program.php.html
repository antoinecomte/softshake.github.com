Program


The program will be split into four themes or tracks, for each of them we dedicate at least one room in order to ensure a perfect balance, a good representation and avoid a theme predominance.
For this year, we have identified three themes :
  • agile
  • iPhone
  • java

We have even included am incubator track in order to leave the floor to speakers on any other computer subject.

Sessions can take various formats (workshops, feedback, state of the art, ...) with different durations, in English or French.

 

The final agenda will be published soon, you can find a list of sessions available here.
