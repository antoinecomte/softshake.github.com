THE CONFERENCE 2013

 

After the success of the previous edtion, Softshake is back for the 3rd edition : Softshake 2013 will take place on thursday 24th and friday 25th octobre, in Geneva at the hepia.

We are expecting more than 300 attendees, both english and french speakers.

This conference is organized thanks to the implication of the local community :

  • The group of iPhone developers of romand Switzerland
  • The Geneva JUG
  • Agile Geneva
  • The Ruby Brigade of Geneva
A conference room is dedicated for each track of the conference, in order to preserve the balance and representation of each of them. For this new edition the tracks are the following :
  • Agile
  • Mobile
  • Java
  • Microsoft
  • Ruby

We also have the Incubator track for every speaker that want to talk about software development on a topic that does not fit into a specific track.
