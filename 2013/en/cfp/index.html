Call for papers is open. The deadline for your submission is Sunday July 14th 2013.

Some precisions :

  • Sessions are mainly in french, or in English
  • Every slot is one hour long. However a session can cover more than one slot.
  • The format of the session is free (academic, workshop, interactive ...): astonish your audience!
  • The speaker agrees to provide a PDF version of its material, in order to allow Soft-Shake.ch to provide it freely to the visitors of the conference.
  • The speaker access freely to the whole conference. In case of a session with more than one speaker, only the main speaker is given a free pass.
  • The tight budget of the conference prevents us to cover speakers travel expenses. We thank you in advance for your understanding.

You can submit sessions for any of the identified tracks :

  • Agile (track-agile@soft-shake.ch)
  • Java (track-java@soft-shake.ch)
  • Microsoft (track-microsoft@soft-shake.ch)
  • Mobile (track-mobile@soft-shake.ch)
  • Ruby (track-ruby@soft-shake.ch)

And you can also submit a session for the Incubator track which is meant to welcome every session that does not fit in another track. Do not hesitate to submit your session : as long as it concerns something related to software development (technologies, practices, methods...), there are no off-topic sessions !

If you have any questions regarding a particular track, please contact directly the track owners via the correct email specified above and using track-incubator@soft-shake.ch for the Incubator.
For any other question, you can contact the organisation team : orga@soft-shake.ch

All the staff is hoping to see you soon.
